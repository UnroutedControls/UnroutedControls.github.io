Log. Run

Last Road to Cinder

Gun run to the final colonial foothold, Region V

Trucks Out 3
Trucks In 3
Ambush Contact Yes
Cargo Delivered Full

Had to do a gun run to Cinder Mill. It's our final foothold in the region — if it falls, we lose the whole salient. Stress was high before we even rolled out. Intel had the main road compromised so we staged from the secondary depot and hoped nobody was watching the ridge.

They were. About halfway in, tracers started walking up the left flank. Driver in truck two radioed he was taking fire and already rerouting north through the tree line. Lost comms for about four minutes — longest four minutes I've sat through in this war. Got them back right as they punched through to the alternate crossing.

We made it. All three trucks. Pulled into the stockpile yard with guns still warm and offloaded everything before the next artillery cycle came in. Cinder Mill held the line that night.
